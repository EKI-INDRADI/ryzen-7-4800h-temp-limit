Underclock using apps / windows only ( not work for gaming )

Open Registry Editor
1. Open your Windows Power Option setting or search "choose a power plan"
2. Choose "your_power_plan_name" or Create a power plan - "your_power_plan_name" Balanced or High performance - OK
3. Click "your_power_plan_name" & click Change advanced power settings
4. Click Processor power management
5. Click Maximum processor state set value 75% (on battery & Pluggin in) or u want - > OK
6. Choose "your_power_plan_name"
